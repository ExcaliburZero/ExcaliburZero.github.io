DevientART

I have been a regular user on DevientART since January 13, 2013. I am a member of a number of groups on DevientART including numerous Pokémon, Doctor Who, Warehouse 13, and general digital arts groups. I regularly submit the artwork that I make to DevientART, this includes renders of 3D models, renders of vector images, sprites, other digital art, photographs, and a few drawings.


Nintendo Wiki

I am a bureaucrat (head admin) of the Nintendo Wiki hosted by Wikia. I have been a dedicated editor of the wiki since I joined it in August 2011. I was eventually promoted to the position of admin in January 2012, and I was later promoted again to the position of bureaucrat in December 2012. Ever since my promotions I have become a dedicated admin of the wiki by removing vandalism and helping new users. Throughout my adminship I have worked hard to improve the wiki by arranging affiliateships with other wikis and working on coming up with new features to improve the wiki.
